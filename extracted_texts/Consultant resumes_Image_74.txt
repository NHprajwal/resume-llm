Consulting In

INTERVENTIONS FOR THE INTRACTABLE, INFLEXIBLE AND IMPULSIVE:

This session will provide teachers with a brief overview of the problem solving model and
then supply teachers of all grade levels with straightforward, classroom tested, ready to use
behavioral interventions for managing and motivating students.

POSITIVE BEHAVIORAL INTERVENTION STRATEGIES

This session addresses positive behavioral intervention strategies (MN Statutes 122A.09).
Participants will understand why problem behaviors occur and then create effective
intervention plans that will impede problem behaviors, teach new skills, and create support
systems for the student.
